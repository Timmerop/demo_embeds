Our tasting room is currently open on Fridays from 4pm to 10pm, Saturdays from noon to 10pm, and Sundays from noon to 9pm. We offer brewery tours at 6pm on Fridays and 1pm, 3pm, and 5pm on Saturdays and Sundays.

Tours are free, open to all ages, and do not require a reservation. Guests over 21 are welcome to purchase beer, wine, cider, and/or sak√© from our tasting room menu to drink at our brewery or take home (we accept cash and credit). Please scroll down to see our full tasting room beer menu and bottles to go list. There is no obligation to purchase any beer to take the tour.

Please note, we do not allow party buses and have limited parking for other large vehicles, which must be reserved in advance. To make arrangements for large vehicle parking, please email tr@jesterkingbrewery.com at least 48 hours prior to your visit. Unfortunately, private tours and group visits outside of our posted tasting room hours are not possible at this time.
