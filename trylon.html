  • About Trylon
    Trylon is a 501(c)3 nonprofit operating the Trylon microcinema and partnering with the the Heights and the Riverview theaters.

    The microcinema is our year round revival cinema, with 50 deluxe rocker seats, a 20 foot screen and both 35mm film and DCP digital pro- jection capability.
