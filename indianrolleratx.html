About Us

Lovingly dubbed a Boutique Roadhouse by friends, the Indian Roller features a warm and eclectic mix of chandeliers, taxidermy, and Americana decor. We are owned and operated by Austinites dedicated to creating a welcoming community space filled with down-home good times in Deep South Austin. Fresh seasonal cocktails and local beers are our specialty.

We are located at 10006 Manchaca Road. We are 21+ with a valid ID. Our hours are 3pm – 2am seven days a week. Come have a drink and stay for some great times.

For booking for private parties and events, please contact indianrollerevents@gmail.com
