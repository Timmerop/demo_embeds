We get paid to go out and drink beer

We love getting out and sharing our work with the community. If we’re not at the brewery making great beer we’re most likely hosting events at local pubs, beer shops or festivals. Stop by for a cold can or pint, and introduce yourself: we’d like to meet you and talk more about brewing beer in a town we love.
